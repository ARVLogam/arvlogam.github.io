Who Are We?

CV. ARV LOGAM is an innovative company engaged in the management and recycling of metal Scrap, with a primary focus on the collection, processing, and provision of quality metal scrap for the scrap processing industry. Founded with a vision to support circular economy practices and environmental sustainability, the company plays a vital role in the metal recycling supply chain.

VISION

To be a leading metal waste provider that supports environmental sustainability through recycling and efficient use of materials.

MISION

Developing an efficient and environmentally friendly metal waste management system, Minimizing negative impacts on the environment through strict processing processes, Ensuring the highest quality standards in the storage and processing of metal scrap, Implementing comprehensive occupational safety and health protocols, Educating the public about the importance of metal recycling.

Why Choose Us?

  • Having a wide network of partners from factories, workshops, construction, and manufacturing industries
  • Ensuring strict quality and material hygiene standards
  • Helping industries achieve environmental sustainability targets
  • Commitment to environmental standards
